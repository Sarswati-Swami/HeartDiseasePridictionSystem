What is Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to conditions affecting heart and blood vessels. It is one of the leading causes of death but can be prevented.

Common Causes

  • High cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Obesity

Symptoms

  • Chest pain
  • Breathlessness
  • Fatigue
  • Dizziness
